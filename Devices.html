    • Some of the most common forms of mobile computing devices are as given below:
    1-Portable computers, compact, lightweight units including a full character set keyboard and primarily intended as hosts for software that may be parameterized, such as laptops/desktops, smartphones/tablets, etc.
    2-Smart cards that can run multiple applications but are typically used for payment, travel and secure area access.
    3-Mobile phones, telephony devices which can call from a distance through cellular networking technology.
    4-Wearable computers, mostly limited to functional keys and primarily intended as incorporation of software agents, such as bracelets, key-less implants, etc.
    Other types of mobile computers have been introduced since the 1990s, including the:
    1-Portable computer
    2-Personal digital assistant/Enterprise digital assistant (discontinued)
    3-Ultra-Mobile PC (discontinued)
    4-Laptop
    5-Smartphone and tablet computer
    6-Wearable computer
    7-E-reader
